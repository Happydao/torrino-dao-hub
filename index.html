How to join Torrino DAO

It’s simple: hold at least one NFT from either collection. Owning any single NFT grants access to the Discord members-only chats. Your treasury share differs depending on which collection you hold.

  • Step 1 — Get an NFT: choose one of the two collections (both valid for membership).
    Buy Gen1 — Torrino DAO (500) Buy Gen2 — Solnauta (888)
  • Step 2 — Join Discord: head to #tutorial to get set up and become part of the community.
    Join Discord
